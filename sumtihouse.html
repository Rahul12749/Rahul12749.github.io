Sumti House

A recess in the midst of coastal nature

ABOUT

A warm and inviting atmosphere has been created by the designer using walls covered in stone, and veneer. With a monochrome background of interior decor, furniture is interpreted as distinct elements in the room.

Bold grey sofa and garnet accent chairs along with a same toned partition all fit together and complement each other. The centrepiece of the residence is the intricately hand-carved marble mandir.

Design Firm:

Alps Interior & Radius.in

Location:

Shreepad Anantta, Surat

Project Size:

3800 sqft

The accent wall in the master bedroom is covered in imported marble and veneer. A coloured panel with a wooden inlay is on the side wall. The blue accent panel and flooring selection give this room for the son a vertical push and depth. The space's powerful and dramatic liveliness is purposely muted by the choice of a monotone colour scheme. The interiors are clutter-free and have simple, clearly defined forms. An effortlessness comes from an approach that is sincere and honest.

This home's interior design is a lesson in restraint, highlighting the significance of space, lighting, and furniture. It is toned down to the bare essentials so that the client is left with a selection of carefully picked items that make the most impact. Yet there is no doubt that it is nothing less than a regal retreat, with each component resonating gracefully and mixing together well. The interior elements like the modern bold furniture, the wall panels, the ceilings, and the light fixtures all together are designed to celebrate this spaciousness within the Interior Space.
