Chandak

A recess in the midst of coastal nature

ABOUT

According to the principal designer, the clients had a strong preference for greys and browns, therefore we wanted to combine those colours in a subtle yet noticeable way. An outstanding living and dining area with an open floor layout makes it easier to picture imagining social events.

The underlying colour scheme is neutral, but vibrant d√©cor touches like carpets and decor items bring in fresh vibrancy. The walls were painted a light grey colour, and the living room and dining room were combined, giving the impression that the area was larger.

Design Firm:

Alps Interior & Radius.in

Location:

Meghrath, Surat

Project Size:

2200 sqft

The light marble flooring and the off-white fluted and black tiled panel create a more open and relaxing atmosphere. This is further accentuated by the veneer-covered entry wall and closet in the master bedroom. This adaptable and progressive environment curated for the daughter masterfully strikes with a bold and bright energy thanks to its minimalist design in muted shades of grey and mauve that is enlivened by splashes of colour and texture in the form of antiques and paintings.

This pleasant bedroom's design was expertly planned to reflect the son's personality, who is passionate about soccer and has modest taste. Even though each room has its own unique aesthetics, all the parts of the house work together beautifully to make a gorgeous house. With a variety of artistic textures, the walls give off an aesthetic appearance and feel, which helps to promote calmness and zen. With its walls covered in a light grey hue, the area anchors a uniform aspect and aesthetically unifies the space.
